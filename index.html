I am a Ph.D candidate in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the University of Delaware

My research interests primarily involve data-intensive studies of transient phenomena, particularly stellar flares, in the era of the Vera C. Rubin Legacy Survey of Space and Time

I am also the project lead of Rubin Rhapsodies, a project to broaden access to the LSST through sound
